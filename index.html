About the Event

Entrepreneurs Meet 2024 is a premier one-day event organized by Entrepreneurs Club Global that aims to bring together entrepreneurs, business leaders, investors, and aspiring innovators to foster growth, collaboration, and networking opportunities. Set in the vibrant city of Kathmandu, this event will focus on empowering local entrepreneurs to think globally and act locally.

The event will explore key topics like startup funding, innovation-driven growth, leadership strategies, and overcoming challenges in entrepreneurship. With a vision to strengthen Nepal’s entrepreneurial ecosystem, the meet promises to be a hub of inspiration, knowledge sharing, and impactful discussions. Whether you're a seasoned entrepreneur or just starting out, this event will provide invaluable insights and opportunities to elevate your entrepreneurial journey.

Why Attend?


"Entrepreneurs Meet 2024" is your chance to learn from experienced entrepreneurs, connect with investors, and engage with thought leaders in the entrepreneurial space. With keynote speeches, interactive workshops, and networking sessions, this meet is designed to inspire, educate, and empower participants to take their ventures to new heights.
